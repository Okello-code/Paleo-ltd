About Us

Paleo Group Limited is a company Based in Kigali, Rwanda, that processes green bananas into green banana flour.

Enhancing consumer health

This is by providing a substitute food product to the traditionally used wheat flour product.

Improving the economic condition

Additionally, this company seeks to improve the economic condition of banana farmers in Rwanda .

Multipurpose product

Going further to providing the consumers with a product with a chocolaty goodness.

Our product is the green banana flour, which is found from processed green bananas. It is a healthier substitute to the wheat flour. Green banana flour has a lighter and fluffier texture rather than gritty and grainy. It has no green banana taste, but a chocolaty goodness. Thus, it can be used to thicken soups and sauces, make bread or even get added to smoothies and noodle recipes.
